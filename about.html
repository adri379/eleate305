About Elevate 305

Connecting students and parents across Florida with opportunities and resources in one place.

Connecting Students to Opportunity

Elevate 305 was created to connect students and parents across Florida with resources that can help them succeed. The goal is simple: making it easier to find opportunities by bringing helpful information into one place. From internships and jobs to schools, student highlights, and even digital portfolios, Elevate 305 helps families discover resources without the stress of searching across multiple platforms.

The best part is that everything is in one place. Instead of spending hours trying to find opportunities, students and parents can explore resources, discover new possibilities, and stay informed from one trusted hub. By taking away the stress of searching, Elevate 305 gives students more time to focus on learning, growing, and succeeding.
